Welcome to Flour & Ferment, your one-stop shop for everything sourdough. Based in the heart of Dublin, Ireland, we’re passionate about helping bakers of all skill levels master the art of sourdough baking. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced baker, we have everything you need to succeed.

Why We Started

Our journey began with a simple love for sourdough. There's something magical about transforming flour, water, and salt into a loaf with complex flavors and a perfectly crisp crust. But quality ingredients and reliable tools make all the difference. That’s why we created a store dedicated to sourdough enthusiasts.

What We Offer

  • Sourdough Starters: Live, active starters, ready to use.
  • Premium Flours: Organic rye, bread flour, and more.
  • Baking Equipment: Bannetons, dough scrapers, and more.
  • Cookbooks: Sourdough recipes from renowned bakers.

Join Our Community

Flour & Ferment is more than a store—it's a community. We love hearing about your sourdough successes and sharing tips. Subscribe to our newsletter for exclusive offers, updates, and new recipes.

Thank you for choosing Flour & Ferment. We’re excited to be part of your sourdough journey!
